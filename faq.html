How do I book museum tickets?

You can book museum tickets in multiple ways:

1. Manual Booking: Go to the Tickets page, select your preferred ticket type (Adult, Child, Senior, Family), choose your visit date and time, enter the number of tickets needed, and complete the payment.

2. Chatbot Booking: Click the chatbot button and say "I want to book tickets" or use the quick booking buttons for instant ticket selection.

3. Demo Accounts: Use demo accounts to test the system - Customer (customer@museum.com), Employee (employee@museum.com), or Manager (manager@museum.com).

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your museum booking with the following policies:

Free Cancellation:
• Up to 24 hours before your visit date
• Full refund to original payment method

Partial Refund:
• 2-24 hours before visit: 50% refund
• Less than 2 hours: No refund

How to Cancel:
1. Go to "My Bookings" in your dashboard
2. Click "Cancel" next to your booking
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Refund processed automatically

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple and secure:

OTP Registration Process:
1. Click "Sign up with OTP" on the login page
2. Choose contact method (Email, SMS, or WhatsApp)
3. Enter your email, phone number, or WhatsApp number
4. Click "Send OTP"
5. Enter the 6-digit OTP you receive
6. Fill in your details (Name, Email, Phone, Password)
7. Agree to terms and conditions
8. Click "Create Account"

Benefits:
• Secure OTP verification via Email, SMS, or WhatsApp
• Save booking history
• Quick rebooking
• Special member offers

What if I don't receive the OTP?

If you don't receive the OTP, try these solutions:

Troubleshooting Steps:
1. Check your email spam/junk folder
2. Verify your phone number is correct
3. Wait 2-3 minutes for delivery
4. Click "Resend OTP" if needed
5. Check internet connection
6. Try different contact method (email vs phone)

Common Issues:
• Email provider blocking automated emails
• Phone number entered incorrectly
• Network delays in SMS delivery
• OTP expired (valid for 10 minutes)

Contact Support:
If issues persist, contact our support team for assistance.

How do QR code tickets work?

QR code tickets provide convenient entry to the museum:

How QR Tickets Work:
1. Book your tickets online or through chatbot
2. Receive QR code via email and in dashboard
3. Save QR code to your phone or print it
4. Show QR code at museum entrance
5. Staff scans the code for validation
6. Receive entry - no physical ticket needed

Benefits of QR Tickets:
• No need to print physical tickets
• Instant delivery to your email
• Environmentally friendly
• Can't lose your ticket
• Easy to share with friends
• Valid for selected date and time

Tips:
• Save QR code to your phone's photo gallery
• Take screenshot as backup
• Ensure phone battery is charged when visiting
• Arrive 10 minutes before your time slot
