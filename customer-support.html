Booking Issues

My booking was cancelled automatically. Why did this happen?

Automatic booking cancellation can occur due to several reasons:

Common Reasons:
1. Payment Timeout: Payment not completed within 15 minutes
2. Payment Failure: Card declined or insufficient funds
3. Session Expired: Booking page left open too long
4. System Maintenance: Scheduled system updates
5. Double Booking: Same ticket booked twice

What to Do:
• Check your email for cancellation reason
• Contact your bank if payment failed
• Try booking again with different payment method
• Use UPI for instant confirmation
• Call support if issue persists

Prevention Tips:
• Complete payment within 10 minutes
• Ensure sufficient funds in account
• Don't refresh payment page
• Use stable internet connection

Booking Issues

My UPI payment failed. What should I do?

If your UPI payment failed, follow these steps:

Immediate Actions:
1. Check if amount was debited from your account
2. Verify UPI app is working properly
3. Check if you have sufficient balance
4. Ensure correct UPI PIN was entered
5. Try again with a different UPI app

If Amount Was Debited:
• Don't try again - you may be charged twice
• Take screenshot of failed transaction
• Contact your bank immediately
• Email us at support@museum.com with details
• Call our support team at +91-XXXXXXXXXX

If Amount Was Not Debited:
• Try booking again with same UPI app
• Check if UPI app is updated
• Try different payment method (Card/Net Banking)
• Ensure stable internet connection
• Clear browser cache and try again

UPI Error Codes:
• "Insufficient Balance" - Add funds to account
• "Invalid UPI PIN" - Reset PIN through your UPI app
• "Transaction Failed" - Try again or contact bank
• "Timeout" - Check internet and try again

Payment Issues

Why was I charged the wrong amount in Indian Rupees?

If you notice incorrect charges in Indian Rupees, check these factors:

Possible Reasons:
1. Currency Conversion: USD to INR rate fluctuation
2. Processing Fees: Bank or UPI app charges
3. International Fees: Cross-border transaction fees
4. Taxes: GST or service charges included
5. Multiple Tickets: Quantity not properly calculated

What to Check:
• Review booking details before payment
• Verify ticket quantity selected
• Check for additional service fees
• Confirm currency conversion rate (₹83.50 per $1 USD)
• Look for hidden charges in final amount

Our Pricing (in Indian Rupees):
• Adult Ticket: ₹30.00
• Child Ticket: ₹15.00
• Senior Ticket: ₹25.00
• Family Package: ₹75.00

Dispute Process:
• Contact us immediately with transaction details
• Provide screenshot of charge amount
• Share booking reference number
• We'll investigate and resolve within 24 hours

Account Issues

I didn't receive the OTP for registration. How can I get help?

If you're not receiving the OTP for account registration:

OTP Troubleshooting:
1. Check your email spam/junk folder
2. Verify phone/WhatsApp number is correct with country code
3. Wait 2-3 minutes for SMS/email/WhatsApp delivery
4. Click "Resend OTP" button if available
5. Try alternative contact method (Email, SMS, or WhatsApp)
6. Check if your email provider blocks automated emails
7. Ensure phone/WhatsApp has good network coverage
8. Verify WhatsApp number is registered and active

WhatsApp OTP Specific:
• WhatsApp must be installed and active
• Phone number must be registered with WhatsApp
• Check WhatsApp notifications and spam folder
• Ensure internet connection is stable

Common Issues:
• Email provider marking OTP as spam
• Phone/WhatsApp number entered incorrectly
• Network delays in SMS/WhatsApp delivery
• OTP expired (valid for 10 minutes only)
• Multiple registration attempts
• WhatsApp business messages filtered

Alternative Registration:
• Call our support team for manual verification
• Visit our museum registration desk
• Use different email address or phone number
• Contact us at: support@museum.com
• Call: +91-XXXXXXXXXX (24/7 support)

Security Note:
Never share your OTP with anyone. Our staff will never ask for your OTP.
